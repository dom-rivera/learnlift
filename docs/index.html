Quizzes with reasons

Instant feedback on every answer — why the correct one is right, and why your pick was wrong.

Cited to your document

Flashcards & scoring

Flip-style cards you self-mark, plus a full score review at the end of every session.

How it works

  1. Open the app and upload your PDF or .docx (up to 20 MB).
  2. Pick Quiz or Flashcards and choose 5–30 questions.
  3. Sign in with a free Claude account when prompted — generation runs on your own account.
  4. Answer, learn from the explanations, and see your score.
